Worked on a project titled A Regulatory DNA Mismatch Repair Network and the Role of MicroRNAs.

Established a boolean model of genetic/protein network interactions of DNA mismatch repair. The network was supported by literature with proteins and miRNAs highly involved in MMR. Some highly involved miRNAs involved are miR-21 and miR-155, and to a lesser extent miR-17/92.

Presented at MBI Capstone Conference at The Ohio State University
